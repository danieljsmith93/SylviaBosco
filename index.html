ABOUT

Sylvia Bosco is a Los Angeles based performing artist, primarily as a vocalist, songwriter, and actress. She has been performing since the age of five, from stage plays to show choirs to hip-hop dance recitals to solo vocal shows. Bosco is a singer/songwriter, accompanied by her acoustic guitar, and plays original material as well as cover songs. Bosco performs regularly at farmer ºs markets, corporate events and community gatherings throughout Southern California. She is currently in the process of writing and recording her upcoming E.P which will be released on all music platforms early 2018. Her musical influences include Carole King, Tom Petty, The Beatles, Adele, and Eric Clapton. Her style is a combination of folk, soul, and blues infused into heartfelt pop melodies. She is studying music at California College of Music including theory, recording, songwriting, and song analysis.
